Checklist

  • No manual conversions—use the selectors.
  • Use serum (not plasma) values if both are present.
  • Calculated osmolality is valid for most adult patients.

When labs mismatch

  • Compare with measured osmolality to estimate osmolar gap.
  • Large gaps may suggest toxic alcohols or lab error.
  • Re-run the strip if any results look implausible.
