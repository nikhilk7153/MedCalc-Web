Input A

Measured sodium

Use the lab-reported unit (mEq/L or mmol/L).

Input B

Serum glucose

Enter the same glucose that prompted the correction.

Formula reminder

  • Corrected Na = measured Na + 1.6 × ((glucose − 100) / 100)
  • The engine handles mmol/L ↔ mg/dL logic automatically.
  • Use only serum values; plasma or whole blood inputs aren’t interchangeable.
