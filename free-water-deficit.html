Hypernatremic range 100–200 mEq/L.

Total body water (TBW) is estimated from sex and age; the calculator derives this automatically.

Formula

  • Deficit = TBW × (1 - 140 / Na)
  • TBW ≈ weight × 0.6 (men) or 0.5 (women)
  • Adjust coefficient to 0.45 for elderly women

Tips

  • Use actual weight, not ideal body weight.
  • Only enter sodium from the same draw as weight.
  • Replace deficit slowly unless otherwise directed.
